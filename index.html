Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Metris

What data sources does Metris integrate with?
Metris connects to all major inverter brands including SolarEdge, Enphase, Huawei, SMA, and more. We also integrate with weather APIs, utility meters, and financial systems to give you a complete picture of your portfolio performance.
How long does it take to get started?
Most customers are up and running within a week. Our team handles the data integration setup, and you'll have access to your dashboard within days. We provide full onboarding support to ensure you get the most out of the platform.
What size portfolios does Metris support?
Metris is built to scale. Whether you're managing 10 sites or 10,000, our platform handles it effortlessly. We currently manage over 5,000 sites and 70+ MW in assets for our customers across the globe.
How does the AI-powered alerting work?
Our AI continuously monitors your portfolio for anomalies, comparing actual performance against expected output based on weather, historical data, and system specifications. When something's off, you get an intelligent alert with context, not just raw data.
Can I generate investor reports automatically?
Yes! Metris generates professional investor reports automatically. You can customize the format, schedule recurring reports, and share them directly with stakeholders. No more spending hours compiling data in spreadsheets.
